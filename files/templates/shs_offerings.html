Field Subject

Field subjects appear in all sections automatically. The semester field on the subject controls which term(s) they appear in:

  • 1st Term or 2nd Term → shown in 1st & 2nd Term schedules (e.g. Research Methods, In-Campus Field Exposure)
  • 3rd Term → shown in 3rd Term only (e.g. Work Immersion)

To change when a Field subject appears, edit the subject's Term Scope in Subjects → Edit.

Core subjects are automatically offered to all sections for their grade. Electives must be assigned to specific sections. Field subjects (e.g. Work Immersion, Research) follow the subject's term scope setting.
